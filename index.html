✅ Do these NOW — before your doctor visit. Twice daily.

#1
Quad Sets
Target: Quadriceps activation
3 sets
15 reps
Hold 10 sec
2× daily
How to do it
1
Sit or lie with leg straight
2
Roll small towel under knee
3
Squeeze quad, push knee into towel
4
Hold 10 seconds, then relax
#2
Straight Leg Raises
Target: Quads + hip flexors
3 sets
12 reps
Hold 5 sec
2× daily
How to do it
1
Lie flat on your back
2
Bend good leg, keep injured leg straight
3
Squeeze quad first, then lift to 45°
4
Hold 5 seconds, lower slowly
#3
Calf Raises
Target: Calves + impact absorption
3 sets
15 reps
Hold 2 sec
Daily
How to do it
1
Stand holding wall for balance
2
Rise slowly on tiptoes
3
Hold 2 seconds at top
4
Lower slowly and controlled
#4
Glute Bridges
Target: Glutes + knee alignment
3 sets
12 reps
Hold 5 sec
Daily
How to do it
1
Lie on back, both knees bent
2
Feet flat, hip-width apart
3
Squeeze glutes, lift hips until straight
4
Hold 5 seconds, lower slowly
#5
Clamshells
Target: Hip abductors + knee stability
3 sets
15 reps
Hold 3 sec
Each side
How to do it
1
Lie on side, hips stacked
2
Knees bent at 45°, feet together
3
Lift top knee like a clamshell opening
4
Hold 3 seconds, lower slowly
#6
Side-Lying Leg Raises
Target: Hip abductors
3 sets
12 reps
Hold 3 sec
Each side
How to do it
1
Lie on side, body straight
2
Toes pointing forward
3
Lift top leg to ~45°
4
Hold 3 seconds, lower slowly
#7
Prone Heel Slides
Target: Hamstrings + range of motion
3 sets
10 reps
Hold 5 sec
Daily
How to do it
1
Lie on your stomach, legs straight
2
Slowly bend injured knee
3
Bring heel toward glutes — only as far as comfortable
4
Hold 5 seconds, straighten slowly

⚠️ Start ONLY after doctor clearance (weeks 4–8)

#8
Short Arc Quads
Target: Quads end range
3 sets
15 reps
Hold 5 sec
How to do it
1
Lie on back, roll towel under knee (bent ~40°)
2
Straighten knee from that bent position only
3
Hold 5 seconds at top
4
Lower slowly back to start
#9
Wall Sits (Shallow)
Target: Quads + glutes
3 sets
Hold 30 sec
20–30° only
How to do it
1
Stand with back flat against wall
2
Slide down to 20–30° knee bend ONLY
3
NOT a full squat — very shallow
4
Hold 20–30 seconds
#10
Terminal Knee Extensions
Target: Quads + ACL support
3 sets
15 reps
With band
How to do it
1
Loop resistance band around fixed object
2
Loop other end behind your knee
3
Stand facing anchor point
4
Straighten knee against band, hold 2 sec
#11
Single Leg Glute Bridge
Target: Glutes + stability
3 sets
10 reps
Injured leg
How to do it
1
Same as glute bridge position
2
Lift good leg off floor
3
All weight through injured leg
4
Lift hips, hold 5 sec, lower slowly
#12
Standing Hamstring Curls
Target: Hamstrings + ACL protection
3 sets
12 reps
Slow & controlled
How to do it
1
Stand holding wall for balance
2
Bend injured knee, bring heel toward glutes
3
Hold 3 seconds at top
4
Lower slowly and controlled
✅ During Exercise
💪
Muscle burn = okay, keep going
🛑
Joint pain = stop immediately
⚠️
Swelling after = you did too much, rest
🦵
Train both legs — not just the injured one
❌ Avoid Completely
🚫
Running or jogging
🚫
Deep squats or lunges
🚫
Cycling — too much knee flexion
🚫
Hiking or uneven terrain
🚫
Sudden pivoting or direction changes
🚫
Sitting cross-legged
🚨 See Doctor Urgently If
🔴
Knee locks completely and won't straighten
🔴
Sudden significant swelling returns
🔴
Sharp pain at rest
🔴
Knee gives way while just walking
🔴
Numbness or tingling in leg

🛋️
